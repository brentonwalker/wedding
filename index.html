Our Story

August 10, 2013 | First Date

Who knew that dinner at Tepanyaki, playing at Boondocks, and smiley face bouncey balls would start it all?

August 16, 2013 | Rodeo, Stargazing, & Ice Cream

Need we say more?

August 24, 2013 | Ferris Wheel

Neither Tiffany nor Brenton cared much for Ferris wheels. Then, all that changed when they held hands on one.

September 20, 2013 | Planetarium

Sneaking into Brenton's room, Tiffany glued a galactic cluster of over 200 stars on the walls and ceiling. Little did he suspect that she would surprise him with a membership to the planetarium as well.

October 14, 2013 | Park City

What better way to welcome autumn than to go play in the mountains! Enjoying the cool air, occasional snow flurry, and firey leaves, Brenton and Tiffany have a fantastic fall adventure.

October 19, 2013 | Santaquin

Ignoring allergies, the couple enjoyed riding around Santaquin on bails of hay towed by John Deere tractors. All the while munching on apples, scones, and enjoying the rustic countryside.

November 30, 2013 | Paper Snowflakes

What did Brenton get when he decided to date an Idaho girl born on Christmas? A lover of all things winter - especially snowflakes. After hours of snowflake creation instruction by Tiffany, Brenton is finally able to design a passable paper snowflake.

December 25, 2013 | Christmas Miracle

Continuing with his crafty streak, Brenton finds himself giving Tiffany a hand-made blanket for her birthday (which also happens to be Christmas). It must be love!

January 2, 2014 | Jazz game

Tiffany had always been hesitant to bring up the Jazz because it makes Brenton grumpy when they lose. However, all that changes when she offers him front row seats to a game (the Jazz even win!) Maybe the couple should get season tickets next year...

January 4, 2014 | Sledding

How better to celebrate the best powder on earth? Sledding with friends of course!

February 14, 2014 | Chocolates, Flowers... and Trampolines?

On Valentine's day chocolates and flowers are a given. This is especially true when a guy's mother is a chocolatier and his sister is a florist. To catch her off guard, Brenton takes Tiffany flying at the trampoline park.

February 21, 2014 | Olympic Ice Skating

In the spirit of the Olympic games, Brenton and Tiffany present their couples' ice dance routine at the local rec center. The crowds go wild...when they exit the rink!

April 19, 2014 | Tulip festival & Hotdogs

Tiffany loves tulips - especially Brenton's "Two lips"! The couple visits the Thanksgiving Point tulip festival. After which Brenton introduces Tiffany to a gourmet meal of chili dogs.

May 17, 2014 | Spontaneous Sidewalk Chalk

On a whim, the couple goes on a walk down Bountiful main street and is pleasantly surprised by abundant local talent. Inspired by the outing, they color the Walker's back yard with chalk a few days later - including the dog!

May 24, 2014 | Local Tourists

Deciding to be local tourists in their own city, the couple visits the Salt Lake Library, City Creek Mall, and the Capitol Building. Piggy back rides around the City County Building are the best!

May 30, 2014 | Engagement

Temple Square has always been a central part of the couple's courtship. After weeks of planning Brenton decides it's time to pop the question. He takes Tiffany to a dinner at The Roof restaurant overlooking the Salt Lake Temple. Throughout the evening he gives her small mementos from their dates: a snowflake, the rodeo ticket stub, a star, and of course the smiley bouncy ball that started it all.
After dinner, Tiffany thinks it is her idea to head to the roof of the Conference Center across the street to view the cityscape. Little does she know that Brenton has an elaborate scheme. Arriving on the roof, and in the sprinkling rain, Brenton gets down on one knee and asks Tiffany to be his eternal companion.
She says yes! She is so excited that she tells every person the couple encounters that evening.

June 4 - 8, 2014 | Disney World

As if the engagemenet isn't exciting enough, Brenton secretly plans with his family to take Tiffany on trip to Disney World. He coordinates the trip details with her boss and coworkers. Needless to say, the Magic Kingdom turns out to be magical!

August 14, 2014 | Sealing

This is where our "Happily Ever After" begins! We're excited that you'll continue to share in our adventures and we can't wait for what the future holds.
